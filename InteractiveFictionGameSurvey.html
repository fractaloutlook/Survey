Thank you for agreeing to take part in our survey!

We have four new stories in development, and we would love to get your opinion on how much they pull you into the experience. These stories are interactive fiction in the same vein as games such as Storyscape, Choices, Romance Club, or Episode: you will choose a name and the appearance of your character, and then jump into the story. Your choices throughout the story will affect your connections with other characters (including romantic options) and the events of the story as you weave your way through the narrative.

Currently, these stories are early in development and do not yet have any art. You will be reviewing the writing by itself. We have removed the customization features for choosing your name and appearance for now.

Below you will find links to a playable version of the first 5-7 minutes of each story as well as a link to a short survey about it. Please review the story then complete its survey.

4 stories. 4 surveys. Read the story and then complete the survey on it. Please complete all four if you can, to be fair to the writers. They love feedback.

Thanks again and we really appreciate your help!

-The Writers
